Spontaneity's Worst Nightmare

PLAN IT

Make a plan for any kind of adventure! Tell us where you want to start, then search for stops to make along the way using Google Places, or create some custom ones of your own. Whether you're planning to climb a mountain before heading to the airstrip for skydiving lessons, or spending an afternoon at the museum then meeting a friend for dinner, Plan-It is a great way to jot down your itinerary.

SHARE IT

Invite your friends or family to make a plan with you! When you share, they will receive an email with a link to your plan. Once they create an account they will be able to collaborate with you by adding their own stops to the plan and leaving comments in real time.

EXPLORE IT

Get out there and live the adventure you planned together!

What Is Plan-It?

Plan-It is a senior capstone project for CMPS 183 - Web Applications by Kevin Mai, Shrey Valia, and Jordan Zalaha using the web2py framework.
